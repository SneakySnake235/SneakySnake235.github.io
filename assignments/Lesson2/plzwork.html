PARC: Repitition

Virgin.com

Virgin

Using their repeated color pallet of red white and grey, the site manages to find interesting ways to style and navigate through their site.

PARC: Promixity

Newgrounds.com

Newgrounds

This site has good Proximity! Newgrounds is a website filled with content that all sorts of creators use to share projects, animated short films, and games. Naturally they would look for a site that focusses on a clear and easy to use platform. Its able to search through thousands of uploads and features rating/scoring and commenting. This site does it all and the spacing for it all is incredible.

PARC: Alignment

Buzzfeed.com

Buzzfeed

Designed to help you scroll through endless posts, comics, and articles. Buzzfeed offers an interesting take on alignment as it flows from one thing to another. The site scrolls on to tabs for articles or videos comics ect. and lets the user scroll down to find something that would potentially interest you.
