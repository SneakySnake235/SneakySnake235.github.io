Zip-Forecast

Site Purpose:

Target Audience: The target audience for this website are for those who are quickly looking to get an update on weather and other information related to the Forecast. This site if for those who love a quick and simple weather update thats accurate and informative.

Personas:

Target Audience:

* Younger teens: For teens who look on their phones before school to decide what clothes should be worn.

* College Students: College students who want to plan a weekend plan or trip and look at weather for accurate information.

Scenarios:

1. Checking weather to make plans for the day

2. Looking at accurate and updated weather for week/weekend

3. Look at site to double check for updates

4. Simple and functional layout makes for fast and easy information

5. Best way to receive updates and information
